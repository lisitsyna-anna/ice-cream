how it’s made?

At IceCream, we start with high quality ingredients. We partner with American farmers for 100% milk and cream that comes from cows not treated with artificial growth hormones.

One of the main ingredients of the dessert is berries and fruits, which are harvested at the height of the season. And exotic flavors of ice cream are created on the basis of fruits that are ordered from European and American suppliers.That’s the reason why we call it natural ice cream.

More about us

The secret of our ice cream lies in the right proportion, love and the main mission: delicious and natural ice cream can be accessible to everyone.

  • step 1

    Preparation of raw materials from which the product will later be made.

  • step 2

    Mixing all the necessary components, in accordance with the recipe, adding fruits.

  • step 3

    Cooling and ripening of the mixture, saturation of ice cream with air.

  • step 4

    Storage in special containers, preparation for sale.

  • step 5

    Adding various flavors at the request of the buyer (syrup, chocolate, nuts, etc.).

  • step 6

    Enjoy the taste of ice cream.
